At SpydraWeb, we strive to deliver high-quality web development and digital services. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which refunds and cancellations are handled.

1. Project Cancellation

a) Before Work Begins

If a project is cancelled before work has started, a full refund may be issued, excluding any transaction or processing fees.

b) After Work Has Started

  • The advance payment is non-refundable.
  • The client must pay for work completed up to the cancellation date.
  • Completed deliverables will be shared only after pending dues are cleared.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only under the following conditions:

  • SpydraWeb is unable to start the project within the agreed timeframe without valid reason.
  • The service delivered significantly deviates from the agreed project scope (after review).

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after project initiation
  • Delays caused by lack of client communication
  • Failure to provide required content or credentials
  • Hosting, domain, or third-party tool purchases
  • Completed and approved milestones

3. Milestone-Based Projects

  • Payments made for completed milestones are non-refundable.
  • Refund requests apply only to pending or unstarted milestones.

4. Subscription or Maintenance Services

  • Clients may cancel future services with written notice.
  • No refund will be provided for the current billing cycle once service has started.
  • Services will continue until the end of the paid period.

5. Domain, Hosting & Third-Party Costs
